Title: Digital presence recording UX

Description: <p>In early December 2020, I was asked to improve the UI/UX of the current screens used to record present staff and guests in buildings of Flensburg University of Applied Sciences. I aligned the different screens with the corporate design and modified the overall design to be touch-friendly. I also added a home button to every page to be able to recover faster after a failure of the system, where the user had to wait up to 10 seconds before it refreshed the screen. Using proper input types made typing the personal numbers way easier.</p><p>In consultation with <a href="https://hs-flensburg.de/hochschule/personen/zimmermann">Uwe Zimmermann</a>, who initiated the UI/UX improvement project, I submitted the proposed changes, which were then deployed in late December, shortly after lauching the original digital recording system.</p>
